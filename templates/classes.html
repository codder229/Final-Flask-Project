My Favorite Class

My favorite Class this semester was probably Multivariable Calculus. Although the class was long and a big grueling, when I think back on how much I learned I am kind of amazed. The topics we covered were also very applicable to almost any other field, and I saw math in a completely different way. I of course have to give an honorable mention to python though, I have really enjoyed this class as well, both because what we are learning is super applicable, and because I legitimately found the assignments fun.
